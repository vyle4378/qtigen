Step 3: Upload the QTI zip file onto Canvas

As a Classic Quiz
  1. Go to your Canvas course.
  2. Click the “Import Existing Content” button on the home page or select the “Import Course Content” on the course settings page.
  3. Under Import Content and next to “Content Type” select “QTI .zip file” from the pull-down menu.
  4. Click the "Choose File" button and select the downloaded zip file.
  5. Click the "Import" button, wait for the import process to complete, and check the Quizzes link in your course for the quiz.
As an item bank in New Quizzes
  1. Go to your Canvas course.
  2. Click the “Item Banks” button on the left sidebar.
  3. Click the blue “+ Bank” button on the top right corner. Enter any name for the item bank since it will be overridden.
  4. Find the item bank in the list and click on it.
  5. Click on the 3 dots on the top right corner of the item bank and select "Import Content".
  6. Click the "Choose File" button and select the downloaded zip file.
  7. Click the "Import" button, wait for the import process to complete, and check that the questions are successfully imported.
